About MindMekka

MindMekka is the builder-focused studio powering actionable playbooks, apps, and systems for creators, solopreneurs, and indie entrepreneurs who want to build better businesses faster.

With nearly two decades of digital product experience and eight years guiding individuals through new software, we combine modern skill stacks, automation, and creative strategy to turn ideas into resilient operations.

No-Code Creators Club, Indie Author Club, Creator Systems Lab, and other self-paced labs.

What drives us

We thrive on shipping experiments, documenting learnings, and sharing them via blog posts, free resources, and community sessions. Our goal is to make advanced automation, AI, and content systems accessible for people who are running one-person businesses or small teams.
