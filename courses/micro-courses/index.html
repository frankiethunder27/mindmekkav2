AI Skill School

Go beyond the hype and learn to build, automate, and scale with artificial intelligence. AI Skill School is a practical, hands-on learning programme designed for creators and entrepreneurs who need to get things done. No fluff, just functional skills.

Get Notified When We Launch

What You'll Learn

We focus on the practical application of AI, teaching you how to integrate powerful tools into your daily workflows.

Prompt Engineering

Master the art of communicating with AI to get precise, high-quality outputs for any task.

AI Content Systems

Learn to build reliable systems for generating, editing, and distributing content at scale.

Research & Analysis

Use AI to synthesise information, uncover market insights, and make data-driven decisions faster.

Workflow Automation

Connect AI tools to your existing software stack to automate repetitive tasks and save hours every week.

AI for Marketing

Implement AI-driven strategies for copywriting, campaign management, and performance analysis.

Video & Audio with AI

Create and edit professional-grade video and audio content using AI-powered tools.

Ethics & Safety

Understand the responsible and safe use of AI, focusing on data privacy and output verification.

How It Works

Our model is built on three core pillars to ensure you don't just learn, you apply.

Stack-Based Learning

Each module is centred on a specific "stack" or combination of tools to solve a real-world problem, from content creation to customer support.

Weekly Practice Sprints

Move from theory to practice with structured weekly challenges that require you to build and implement what you've learned.

Ship & Feedback

Complete tangible projects and get direct feedback within a community of fellow builders, reinforcing your skills through application.

Learning Tracks

Choose the path that fits your current skill level and future goals.

Beginner Foundations

For those new to AI who need to understand the core concepts and master essential tools.

Builder Workflow

For creators and entrepreneurs who want to integrate AI deeply into their existing business systems.

Pro Operator

For advanced users aiming to build complex automations and leverage AI for strategic advantage.

Frequently Asked Questions

When will AI Skill School launch?

We are targeting a launch in early 2026. Sign up for the waiting list to get specific dates as soon as they are announced.

What will the pricing be?

While exact pricing is not finalised, we plan to offer a one-time purchase for lifetime access to each track, with a significant discount for founding members. Expect a price point in the range of a premium online course.

What is the weekly time commitment?

Each track is designed to be flexible. You can expect to spend 3-5 hours per week to watch the lessons and complete the practical sprints.

What tools will I need?

The programme will use a mix of free and paid AI tools. We prioritise tools with free tiers or trials, but some modules may recommend premium software for advanced workflows. A full tool list will be provided before enrolment opens.

Who is this for (and who is it NOT for)?

This is for builders: creators, entrepreneurs, and small teams who want to apply AI to achieve business outcomes. It is NOT for those seeking a purely theoretical, academic understanding of AI or machine learning.
